Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

This policy explains what personal data Brickwork collects, why, and what your rights are. Brickwork is an independent web-design studio based in the United Kingdom and is the "data controller" for the information described below. We've kept this in plain English.

In short: we only collect what you send us through the contact form, we use it solely to reply and (if you go ahead) deliver your project, we never sell it, and you can ask us to delete it at any time.

What we collect

  • Enquiry details you submit: your name, business name, email address, optional phone number, the service you're interested in, and anything you write in the message field.
  • Technical data: like most websites, our host and our form provider may log standard request data such as IP address and browser type for security and abuse prevention. This site sets no tracking or advertising cookies of its own.

Cookies & local storage

This site sets no cookies. If you use the currency switcher on the pricing section, your choice (e.g. "GBP") is saved in your browser's local storage so the page remembers it next time. That value never leaves your device and identifies nothing about you. All fonts are hosted on our own website, so viewing this site does not cause your browser to contact Google or any other font provider.

How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry and prepare your free mockup.
  • To deliver and support your project if you decide to proceed.
  • To keep records of our agreement and comply with legal obligations.

Our lawful bases under UK GDPR are your consent (when you submit the form), taking steps to enter a contract at your request, and our legitimate interest in running and securing the business.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the service providers needed to operate:

  • Formspree — processes and forwards contact-form submissions to us.
  • Stripe — handles payments if you engage us (Stripe collects payment details directly; we never see your full card number).
  • Our website host (Netlify) — hosts this website and serves it to your browser.

International transfers

Some of the providers above (Formspree, Stripe, Netlify) are based in the United States, so form submissions and payment records may be processed there. These transfers rely on safeguards recognised under UK GDPR — the UK Extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and/or the UK International Data Transfer Agreement / standard contractual clauses — and each provider publishes its own GDPR commitments.

How long we keep it

Enquiries that don't become projects are kept for up to 12 months, then deleted. Records relating to actual projects and payments are kept for up to 6 years to meet UK tax and accounting requirements.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask us to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your data, and to object to processing or request portability. To exercise any of these, contact us (below). You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Contact

For any privacy request, message us via the contact form or on Instagram at @brickworkstudio_.
