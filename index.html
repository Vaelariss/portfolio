FAQ

Common questions
answered.

How does the 50/50 payment work?

+

You pay 50% upfront to secure your project slot. The remaining 50% is only due after you've reviewed the finished site and you're happy with it. All payments go through PayPal, so you have full buyer protection — you'll never be out of pocket.

What do I need to provide?

+

Just a short brief: your brand name, products or services, any colours or logos you have, and a couple of reference sites you like. No technical knowledge needed. I ask you everything in a simple questionnaire that takes around 10 minutes.

What if I need changes after delivery?

+

You get 7 days of free revisions after I hand over the site. If something doesn't look right or needs tweaking, just let me know and I'll fix it. No extra charge for reasonable changes during that period.

Do you actually deliver in 7 days?

+

Yes. The 7-day timeline starts the day after I receive your completed brief. I take on one project at a time so your build gets my full attention. If something delays delivery significantly, I'll communicate it early and offer a partial refund.

Do I need a Shopify subscription?

+

Only if you're selling products online and taking payments through your website. Shopify plans start at around £25/month and you'll set this up in your own name — I'll walk you through it. However, if you're a local business like a jeweller, barber, salon, or restaurant that doesn't need online payments, you do not need Shopify at all. Your brochure site is built with plain HTML/CSS and has no monthly fees.

I'm a local business — do I need online payments?

+

Not necessarily. Most local businesses — jewellers, barbers, salons, restaurants — just need a website that looks professional, shows what they do, and makes it easy to call, find directions, or book. The brochure site package (£350) is built exactly for this. No Shopify, no monthly fees, no complicated setup. Just a great-looking site that works.

How do I get started?

+

DM me on Instagram (@storecraftbuilds) saying you're interested in a Shopify store or brochure site. I'll reply within a few hours with a short brief form. Once you fill it in and pay the deposit, I get started straight away.
